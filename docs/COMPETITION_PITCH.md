# Pravinya Competition Positioning

## Hook
“I scored 31 out of 40 in English. Can anyone tell me why I lost those nine marks?”

Was it concept knowledge? Spelling? Punctuation? Application? Question interpretation?

## Problem
Scores measure outcomes but do not systematically diagnose causes.

## Solution
LearnLens AI converts assessment evidence into a Learning MRI.

## Demo
31/40 → Select assessment → Upload answer sheet → AI analysis → Learning MRI → Root cause → What Next? → Teacher insight → Parent insight.

## Innovation
Not merely AI grading. Assessment evidence becomes structured diagnosis, longitudinal learning intelligence, and actionable next steps.

## Entrepreneurship
Customer: schools.  
Users: students, teachers, parents.  
Model: B2B2C SaaS.  
Scale: one school contract can serve hundreds or thousands of students.

## AI
Multimodal understanding + rubric-grounded reasoning + diagnostic classification + longitudinal pattern analysis + personalized next actions.

## Closing
“LearnLens doesn't stop at telling students where they lost marks. It helps them understand how to win those marks back.”
