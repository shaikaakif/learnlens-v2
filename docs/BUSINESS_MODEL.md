# Business Model

## Model
B2B2C EdTech SaaS.

Buyer: School/institution.  
Users: Students, teachers, parents, administrators.

## Value
Student: knows why marks were lost and what to improve.  
Teacher: scalable diagnostic visibility.  
Parent: understandable actionable insight.  
School: differentiated AI-enabled academic intelligence.

## Revenue Possibilities
- one-time onboarding/integration,
- annual SaaS subscription,
- included AI-analysis allowance,
- additional usage,
- optional white-label/integration services.

## Costs
AI inference, hosting, database, storage, support, engineering, security, onboarding.

## Go-To-Market
Demonstration → one-class/subject pilot → validate accuracy/usefulness → collect teacher feedback → expand within school → approach school groups.

Do not finalize arbitrary pricing without unit-economics and willingness-to-pay validation.
